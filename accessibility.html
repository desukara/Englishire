Accessibility Statement

Englishire aims to provide a website that is perceivable, operable, understandable and robust across current browsers, devices and assistive technologies.

The website is developed with semantic HTML, keyboard access, visible focus states, descriptive link text, alternative text for meaningful images and responsive layouts. Englishire uses WCAG 2.2 Level AA as its working reference rather than claiming formal certification.

Some historic images, third-party services or older documents may present limitations. We review reported barriers and prioritise practical corrections.

If any page, form or document is difficult to use, email info@englishire.com and identify the page, device, browser and difficulty encountered. We will provide the information in a reasonable alternative format where possible.

This statement was last reviewed on 22 July 2026.
