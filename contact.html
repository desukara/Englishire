Common Enquiry Questions

Practical answers before you send the form.

These answers concern the enquiry itself. The full questions page contains further information about temporary teacher cover.

What if the timetable is not yet final?

Provide the current version and state clearly that it remains provisional. We will review any material change before final confirmation.

May we send supporting documents separately?

Yes. Timetables, lesson plans, school maps and other useful documents may be supplied after the initial enquiry using the communication details in our response.

Does sending an enquiry reserve a teacher?

No. Sending the form begins the review process. A teacher is reserved only after Englishire has expressly confirmed the assignment.

Can we enquire before the need becomes urgent?

Yes. An advance introduction gives us useful context and can make a later request easier to assess.
