Englishire Journal Editorial Policy

Englishire Journal publishes analysis and commentary on English education, school operations, staffing and continuity. Articles are selected for relevance, clarity and practical value rather than search volume alone.

Authorship is stated on each publication. Sources, institutions, historical landmarks and images are identified where that information is material and can be established responsibly.

Published article prose is preserved as part of the publication record. Material factual errors may be corrected, but substantive corrections should be dated and explained rather than silently altering the record.

Commercial service pages and editorial publications serve different purposes. Englishire Journal may inform the organisation’s work, but articles are not presented as contractual advice or a guarantee of service availability.

Questions, correction requests and attribution concerns may be sent to info@englishire.com.
