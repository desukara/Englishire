The Matter in Plain English

Schools deserve breathing room.

A teacher becomes unwell. A new appointment takes rather longer than expected. A timetable expands before permanent staffing is ready. The pupils still arrive, parents still expect a proper lesson and the school must still conduct its day with composure.

Englishire gives schools time to manage the wider staffing matter carefully, without treating tomorrow's lesson as an excuse for a hurried permanent decision.

Temporary cover should relieve pressure without lowering standards.

When Englishire Steps In

Staffing difficulties seldom arrive at a convenient hour.

Englishire assists when a carefully arranged timetable encounters an entirely unhelpful reality.

01

Unexpected Absence

A member of staff is unavailable and the first lesson is already approaching.

Urgent circumstances may be considered without pretending that urgency removes the need for judgement.

02

Between Appointments

The previous teacher has departed and the right permanent appointment has not yet been made.

Temporary cover gives the school room to recruit carefully rather than appoint merely because Monday is approaching.

03

Additional Capacity

New classes, seasonal programmes or timetable changes have created more teaching hours than expected.

Temporary support allows the school to decide whether the demand is seasonal, developing or permanent.

04

Trials, Events and Limited Programmes

A school may require a teacher for a demonstration lesson, event, intensive course or other defined period.

Not every short requirement ought to become a permanent recruitment exercise.

Designed Around the Actual School

The assignment must be understood before it is accepted.

Every school has its own rhythm. Before confirmation, Englishire considers the timetable, learner ages, class format, lesson expectations, location, travel and the standards by which the school conducts itself.

The object is not to send whoever happens to be free. It is to provide sensible temporary support for the classroom the school actually runs.

Possible duration
One day, several days, recurring weekly cover or a longer temporary period.
Possible settings
English schools, academies, private and international programmes and related education providers.
Possible age groups
Children, teenagers and adults, depending upon suitability and assignment requirements.
Possible lesson types
Group classes, private lessons, conversation classes, trials and special programmes.

How much notice does Englishire require?

More notice generally provides more options, though urgent enquiries are welcome. Availability depends upon the dates, times, location and nature of the assignment.

Can a teacher cover just one day?

Yes. An assignment may concern a single day, recurring weekly cover or a longer temporary placement.

Does making contact guarantee cover?

No. Every assignment must be reviewed before it can be confirmed.

What information should a school provide?

Kindly include the location, dates, timetable, learner ages, class sizes, lesson format and any particular requirements.
