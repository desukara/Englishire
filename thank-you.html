Enquiry Email

If you have sent your enquiry email, thank you for taking the time to contact Englishire.

The contact form prepares an email but does not send it. Once you have reviewed and sent that email from your own email application, Englishire can consider the assignment before responding.

What happens once you send the email?

Every enquiry received by email is read individually so that the school, timetable, learner groups and teaching requirements may be understood properly.

We Read

Every enquiry is considered carefully rather than processed mechanically. The details matter.

We Consider

Availability, suitability, location, timetable and the nature of the assignment are reviewed before any promise is made.

We Reply

Once the assignment has been considered properly, we will respond as soon as reasonably possible with the next appropriate step.

Our Principle

Your enquiry will be reviewed before any cover is confirmed.

We believe that schools deserve thoughtful replies rather than hurried assurances. That principle guides every enquiry received.
